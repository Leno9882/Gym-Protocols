HYPERTROPHY PERFORMANCE INTEL

  • The 3-0-1-0 Tempo: Lower the bar for 3 seconds, no pause at the bottom, 1 second to explode up, no pause at the top. This keeps the chest under 100% load.
  • Stretch-Mediated Growth: On DB Press, go as deep as your shoulders allow. The bottom 2 inches are where the most growth is triggered.
  • Lockout Rule: On Machine Press, do not lock your elbows. Stop 1 inch before lockout to keep the tension on the muscle, not the joints.
