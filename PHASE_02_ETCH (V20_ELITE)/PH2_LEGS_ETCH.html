IMPORTANCE: METABOLIC ETCHING

Mechanical Tension: Keeps the mass. Metabolic Stress: Creates the separation. We achieve this by moving heavy loads through a full stretch with controlled eccentrics.

QUADS: DENSITY
Smith-Machine Squat 4 x 10, 10, 8, 8
SPEC: Feet narrow and low for Outer Sweep (Vastus Lateralis).
Hack Squat (Open Stance) 3 x 12
SPEC: Pause 1s at absolute bottom. No lockout at the top.
Barbell Lunges 3 x 12 Per Leg
SPEC: Lean torso forward 15° to engage the Glute-Ham tie-in.
HAMSTRINGS: ETCHING
Romanian Deadlift 4 x 10
SPEC: Bar must stay against legs. Push hips back until hamstrings "scream."
Seated Leg Curl 3 x 15
SPEC: Pull toes toward shins (Dorsiflexion) to maximize gastrocnemius involvement.
CALVES: DIAMOND
Standing Calf Raise 5 x 15
SPEC: 3s descent. 2s hold at peak. 1s stretch at bottom.

PRO NOTE: THE "GLASS" LOOK

To get that paper-thin skin look (definition), you must force blood into the muscle and keep it there. Do not fully straighten your legs on Quads—keep the tension on the muscle, not the joints.
